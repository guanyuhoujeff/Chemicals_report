Approximately 1-2 tons of the gas, which has the sharp smell of disinfeclant, leaked out, but no deaths or injuries occurred in the plant. The persons who discovered the leak immediately notified the industrial park's fire department and the Bureau of Fnvironmental Protection, and a response center was established. Response personnel wore protective clothing and carried oxygen tanks when they entered the allected area to perform emengency rescue work.
