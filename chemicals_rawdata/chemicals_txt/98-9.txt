The accident was caused by the gas control lever on the top of the gas cylinder contacting rust resulting in a gas leak. The plant initially confirmed an abnormal concentration level of the gas from the detection alarm and carried out personnel evacuation and roll-call, and then notified the government and arca toxic disaster control alliance task force. The scope of impact covered the plant's gas chamber of approximately 3pings. Repeated detection testing confirmed no other leaks.
